Who We Are

Jaybird Properties is a Milwaukee, WI based commercial real estate company specializing in the acquisition and management of premium mixed-use space across the Metro-Milwaukee area. Our team of experienced professionals work diligently to provide the highest level of service to our clients and tenants.

We take a client-driven approach to every aspect of our real estate services. Each client receives prompt attention from our team of professionals. Regardless of your project, we work closely with you to deliver the strategies that will help you meet your current and long-term goals. Through every phase of a project, we are driven by our commitment to excellence, creativity and professionalism.
